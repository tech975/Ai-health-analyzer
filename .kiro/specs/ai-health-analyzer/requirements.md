# AI Health Analyzer Requirements Document

## Introduction

The AI Health Analyzer is a web-based application that enables users to upload patient health reports, receive AI-powered analysis, and manage their health report history. The system provides intelligent interpretation of medical data with patient-friendly explanations, abnormal value detection, and actionable health recommendations.

## Glossary

- **Health_Analyzer_System**: The complete web application including frontend, backend, database, and AI integration
- **User**: Any person accessing the application to analyze health reports
- **Health_Report**: A medical document (PDF) containing patient test results, lab values, or diagnostic information
- **AI_Analysis**: The automated interpretation and summary generated by the AI model based on the uploaded health report
- **Report_History**: The collection of previously generated analyses stored for a specific user
- **Patient_Profile**: The demographic and contact information associated with a health report

## Requirements

### Requirement 1

**User Story:** As a user, I want to upload a patient's health report and receive an AI-generated analysis, so that I can understand the medical information in simple terms.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN a user uploads a valid PDF health report, THE Health_Analyzer_System SHALL accept the file and store it securely
2. WHEN a user provides patient information (name, age, gender, phone), THE Health_Analyzer_System SHALL validate and store the data
3. WHEN a user clicks the generate button, THE Health_Analyzer_System SHALL process the report through AI analysis
4. THE Health_Analyzer_System SHALL generate a comprehensive analysis containing summary, abnormal values, detected conditions, causes, symptoms, lifestyle recommendations, and medical advice
5. WHEN analysis is complete, THE Health_Analyzer_System SHALL display the results on the Report page

### Requirement 2

**User Story:** As a user, I want to download and share my health analysis reports, so that I can keep records and share them with healthcare providers.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. WHEN viewing a generated report, THE Health_Analyzer_System SHALL provide download options for PDF and Word formats
2. WHEN a user selects download, THE Health_Analyzer_System SHALL generate the file within 10 seconds
3. THE Health_Analyzer_System SHALL provide a share functionality for generated reports
4. WHEN sharing a report, THE Health_Analyzer_System SHALL generate a secure shareable link
5. THE Health_Analyzer_System SHALL maintain the original formatting and content in downloaded files

### Requirement 3

**User Story:** As a user, I want to view my previous health report analyses, so that I can track my health trends over time.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Health_Analyzer_System SHALL store all generated reports in the user's history
2. WHEN a user accesses the History page, THE Health_Analyzer_System SHALL display a list of previous reports with ID, patient name, phone number, and generation date
3. THE Health_Analyzer_System SHALL provide search functionality to find specific reports by patient name or date
4. WHEN viewing report history, THE Health_Analyzer_System SHALL provide actions to view, delete, or select multiple reports
5. THE Health_Analyzer_System SHALL implement pagination when more than 10 reports exist

### Requirement 4

**User Story:** As a user, I want to filter and manage multiple health reports efficiently, so that I can organize and maintain my health data.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Health_Analyzer_System SHALL provide filter options by age and gender on the History page
2. WHEN filters are applied, THE Health_Analyzer_System SHALL update the report list in real-time
3. THE Health_Analyzer_System SHALL provide bulk selection with checkboxes for multiple reports
4. WHEN reports are selected, THE Health_Analyzer_System SHALL enable bulk delete functionality
5. THE Health_Analyzer_System SHALL confirm deletion actions before permanently removing reports

### Requirement 5

**User Story:** As a user, I want the application to be secure and reliable, so that my health data remains protected and the system performs consistently.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Health_Analyzer_System SHALL validate all uploaded files to ensure they are valid PDF documents
2. THE Health_Analyzer_System SHALL store uploaded files securely using cloud storage with access controls
3. WHEN processing fails, THE Health_Analyzer_System SHALL display appropriate error messages to the user
4. THE Health_Analyzer_System SHALL complete AI analysis within 30 seconds for standard health reports
5. THE Health_Analyzer_System SHALL maintain data integrity and prevent unauthorized access to health information

### Requirement 6

**User Story:** As a user, I want an intuitive and responsive interface, so that I can easily navigate and use the application on any device.

#### Acceptance Criteria

1. THE Health_Analyzer_System SHALL provide a consistent header with logo, navigation links, and footer across all pages
2. THE Health_Analyzer_System SHALL display forms with clear labels and validation messages
3. WHEN on mobile devices, THE Health_Analyzer_System SHALL adapt the layout for optimal usability
4. THE Health_Analyzer_System SHALL provide visual feedback during file upload and AI processing
5. THE Health_Analyzer_System SHALL maintain accessibility standards for users with disabilities
